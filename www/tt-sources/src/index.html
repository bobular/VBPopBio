Welcome to the new and improved VectorBase Population Biology resource, which is part of our ongoing efforts to integrate genomic, phenotypic (including insecticide resistance) and population data. What's new?

  • Stable IDs for projects, samples and assays (so you can bookmark pages and refer to entries in our database in publications).
  • Much simplified submission procedure
  • Improved data representation and search
  • New clean design

You are very welcome to submit data for any field or laboratory based studies of genotypic and/or phenotypic diversity.

You can view an example project, browse a list of projects, or search using the forms below (or search at any time from the site-wide top-right search box).
